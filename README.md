# DiggiClass

DiggiClass is a comprehensive Revision Tracker and Practice Application designed to help students and learners stay consistent with their study schedules.

## Features
- **Smart Revision Tracking**: Automatically schedules revisions based on spaced repetition intervals (e.g., 1, 7, 14, 30 days).
- **Google Calendar Integration**: Seamlessly syncs your revision sessions with Google Calendar.
- **Push Notifications**: Receive timely reminders for due revisions on all your devices.
- **PWA Support**: Installable on mobile and desktop with offline capabilities.
- **Progress Tracking**: Visualize your study consistency with dashboards and charts.
